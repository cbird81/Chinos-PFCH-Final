Invest In Your Future

IMPACT INVESTING AND MILLENIALS

In the past investing was a topic and activity left for the financial literate and wealthy, however in recent times this has changed. With technology pushing the capabilities of innovative disruptive Start-Ups the door is now opening to more people, allowing them to invest with lower initial investment amounts/capital. One group being sought after in this new opportunity are the millennial generations those in their 20’s - 30’s. As they begin to take the reigns from generations before like Baby boomers, they also take the wealth and look to invest in more progressive ways that they are more accustomed to their way of life like using technology for their financial transactions. Another popular idea among the millennials is the idea of sustainability, finding and supporting initiatives that have a positive impact on the environment and world at large. While there is no universal standard to how this is being achieved or measured, various methodologies and projects that reflect these ideas have been seen to get attention and are gaining adoption and being seen as efforts of best practices. While not all financial institutions have made this their goal a lot are adopting these practices. And those that will look to connect with younger generations will need to continue to find ways to convey their messages in efficient and creative ways. The idea of triple bottom line investing is on the rise where organizations not only think of profit as the bottom line but also people being affected and the way the planet is being impacted. Most companies have started ESG initiatives and find ways to include this in their annual reports.
